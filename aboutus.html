About Us

We are Mamay and Elijah—members of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church and dedicated mezemran (church singers). Our faith, culture, and passion for storytelling inspired us to create Ema Art Studio, a space where moments become memories.

From weddings and graduations to baptisms, birthdays, portraits, and special events, we are committed to capturing life's most meaningful occasions with beauty and authenticity.

At Ema Art Studio, we don't just take photos—we preserve emotions, celebrate heritage, and turn every snapshot into timeless art.
